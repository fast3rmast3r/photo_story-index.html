the now grown up kid, as a student
in highschool. He gets
involved with a bad company.

he stays with them until late hours.
He begins skipping classes
and spends more time with the
bigger kids in parks.

Although less expressed here, we can still see the contrast, that helps us see the
difference between the two students: gray and black. Even though he's darker
than before, he's still shining more than the other boy, who's lost all of the light inside him.
